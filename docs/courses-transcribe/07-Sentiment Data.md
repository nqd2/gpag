# 7-Sentiment Data

Sentiment data quantifies the emotions of the masses towards the stock or market in general. The data are captured from various mediums like social media channels, news, and blogs. Quant researchers monitor popular opinion in an effort to seek to predict the direction of the movement in the stock price and the conviction or the intensity underlying these sentiments. Quant researchers also analyze the sentiment buzz that is the degree of activity of the investors in a particular stock.

If a stock's sentiment buzz is rising compared to historical average, that means the stock is attracting higher investor attention lately and is possibly overpriced so we expect lower future returns and short the stock. Conversely, we go long on stocks with falling buzz anticipating higher future returns.
