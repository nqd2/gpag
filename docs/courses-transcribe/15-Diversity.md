# 15-Diversity

Diversity is the cornerstone of quant research. The reason is simple. The more diverse our signals are, the better the risk-adjusted returns or sharp ratio of our approaches may be. When we combine alphas that have low correlation of less than 0.5, the fluctuation in one alpha's returns are offset by the variations in other low correlated alphas.

This diversity may result in lower volatility for the targeted returns of the portfolio.
