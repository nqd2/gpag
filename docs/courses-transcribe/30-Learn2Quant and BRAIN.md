# 30-Learn2Quant and BRAIN

At WorldQuant, we know that quant finance research starts with curiosity and creativity, with data science and market insights. We also know that becoming a top quant researcher takes more than that. You need the right support from a competitive community, along with the cutting edge tools to shape and deploy your ideas. That's why we created WorldQuant Brain.

Brain is a platform focused both on cultivating quant skills and research development, powering a global connected community of brain research consultants. We believe in development through competition, which is the philosophy of our founder Igor Tulchinsky. Brain is your opportunity to bring your analytical and creative skills together, to learn and practice using quant tools and techniques, to get real-time feedback from experts and peers, to shape the research approaches leveraged. Brain is also fun.

It's gamified and easy to access online. It's both your lab for testing ideas and your battleground, where ideas compete so that everyone can improve. Every month, over 100,000 aspiring quant researchers from more than 150 countries log into WorldQuant Brain to research and test their ideas and see immediate feedback from our sophisticated simulation tools. And throughout the year, we host competitions like university-level alphathons and the International Quant Championship.

Last year, 30,000 participants showcased their skills in the International Quant Championship. Some of the best may receive invitations to apply to be a brain research consultant. Brain research consultants have the opportunity to be mentored by WorldQuant research advisors, continue to participate in competitions to refine their alpha ideas, and even potentially receive compensation for the quality of their ideas. As much as there is to be gained from the mentorship, many consultants get the most out of the community.

Working with a global network of quant researchers, sharing ideas, pushing each other to think boldly, pushing each other to keep going, and pushing WorldQuant to keep innovating. That's why we built Brain, why we are constantly evolving the platform, providing new features, new tools, new data fields to inspire our researchers and give them the power to inspire each other. You can certainly learn more about how you can fully harness the power of Brain and develop your research ideas with me during this video series, Learn to Quant. Anyone can build that future.

One idea, one model, one alpha at a time. Keep experimenting and we will see you there.
